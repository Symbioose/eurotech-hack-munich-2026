## Our Track

Chosen track:

Reason:

- Hong Kong is a credible dense-city testbed.

- The demo is physical and visual without competing directly in the robotics-arm track.

## Hong Kong / GBA Thesis

Hong Kong:

- dense urban testbed
- aging buildings, high-rises, stations, malls, airports, ports
- smart-city operators and pilot opportunities
- trusted front door for European startups entering Asia

Greater Bay Area:

- Shenzhen electronics
- Dongguan enclosures, plastics and metal fabrication
- Guangzhou / Hong Kong compliance and logistics
- EMS and supplier network depth

Core line:

> Hong Kong is the trusted front door. GBA is the manufacturing engine.

## Public Context For BuildGuard

The Buildings Department's Mandatory Building Inspection Scheme applies to private buildings aged 30 years or above, except domestic buildings not exceeding three storeys, when served with statutory notices. Owners must appoint a Registered Inspector for prescribed inspection and any required repair works covering common parts, external walls and projections or signboards.
